Introduction


Maharaja College, Jaipur, is a prestigious institution that has been a cornerstone of higher education in Rajasthan. Affiliated with the University of Rajasthan, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields of Science, Commerce, and Arts. Known for its rich history and academic excellence, Maharaja College has a sprawling campus with modern infrastructure, research facilities, and a vibrant student culture.


Established


One of the oldest colleges in Rajasthan, founded in 1844.
Affiliation University of Rajasthan.


Courses Offered


B.Sc., B.A., B.Com, M.Sc., M.A., M.Com, and several diploma and certificate programs.


Campus


Equipped with state-of-the-art labs, libraries, sports facilities, and a student center.


Student Life


The college promotes extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and academic societies.


Alumni


The college boasts of an illustrious list of alumni who have contributed significantly to various fields such as politics, science, literature, and administration.


Mission & Vision


Maharaja College aims to provide quality education that fosters academic growth, critical thinking, and holistic development of students. The institution strives to uphold its legacy of excellence by adapting to modern educational standards while preserving its rich heritage.
